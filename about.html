How We Work

Our application takes your ingredients, and returns images of each ingredient for verification. Let us know how much time you have to cook. The amount of time and ingredients will then be used to search for recipes. Then you can create with dishes based on ingredients you already have in your kitchen. No one wants to waste food, money, or time.
